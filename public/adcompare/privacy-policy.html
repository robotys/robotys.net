Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AdCompare. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services to analyze Meta Ads performance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

2. Information We Access

AdCompare is a client-side application. We access your Meta Ads data through official Meta Graph APIs only after you grant explicit permission. The data accessed includes:

  • Public Profile: To identify your user session.
  • Ads Insights: Campaign names, adset names, results, spend, reach, and frequency.

3. How Information is Used

The information we access is used strictly to generate the comparison reports within your browser. We do not store, save, or transmit your ad data to any external servers. All calculations and data visualizations happen locally on your device.

4. Data Security

We use the official Meta JavaScript SDK to ensure secure authentication. Your Access Token is stored temporarily in your browser's memory and is destroyed when you log out or close the browser tab.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not store your data on our servers, we have no data retention policy for your Meta Ads insights. Once your session ends, the data is removed from your browser's local state.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the hosting domain (robotys.net).
